Cafe Design

We are pioneers at making your space look like the uber socializing hub. Peppy, trendy, minimalistic or chic – every concept can be turned into designs. Your customers will have a gala time in the spaces decorated by Umber Frame Studio.


Whenever people think of a cafe, they visualize a cool, hip place in their minds. A place with an easy-going aura and a not-too-sophisticated ambience. At Umber Frame Studio, we understand this requirement at the core and can transform your cafe space from head to toe. Your space will be dripping with trendiness. Our team of best interior designers will take your requirements and convert your restaurant space into the most happening cafe on the block. We will never compromise with the mood and style of the space and will always keep it at the highest priority.

Pubs Design

Umber Frame Studio specializes in designing and decorating pubs which go on to become social uber hubs. We have the design mavericks in our team of experts, the best minds in this field, who can work their magic to make your pub, the most sought out destination for socializing. We are well versed with the intricacies that goes into making a pub, and our team of interior designers are the best in Bangalore and the right folks for the job. Nightclub Design

Bling, Glam and Glitter ! We can bring it all, and more to the interiors of your nightclub. We excel in making your space the ultimate hangout spot for the city’s nightlife scene. Whether it’s a DJ section or the dance floor or the dazzling chandeliers or the bar area, our designers and architects can give you all possible interior solutions which will match your expectations and even go beyond. Our commitment to quality and expert designs will convert your night club into a crowd magnet. We take pride in our creations and our interior designers work in conjunction with manufacturers, engineers and contractors to make sure that all the architectural elements and physical structures are aligned in line with the business requirements and vision of the client.

We strive to achieve perfection in design through innovation & creativity.
