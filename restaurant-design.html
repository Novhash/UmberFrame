Fine Dine Design

We will inculcate creativity & trendiness into every nook of your noshery. Our designs will elevate your patron’s experience and bring out the best results for your establishment. Our expert designers understand the hospitality sector and will help revamp your space.


Fine Dining is a word associated with luxury and sophistication. Such a space needs meticulous planning and precision in it’s execution. We create such spaces with high quality materials and implement modern sleek designs. Every single detail is planned to the tee and as per your requirements. Your patrons will enjoy a rich and decadent ambience in the fine dining space designed by our interior decorators. We also excel at designing spaces based on a theme or era. Every idea or vision you have for your restaurant can be turned to reality with our designs. We also provide special attention to the kitchen’s design as it needs to match the tone of the high end interiors. The cooking space is made keeping in mind practicality, functionality as well an artistic touch. We aim to strike a balance between top-notch functionality and aesthetic sense.

Bistro Design

Our interior consultants make bistros to be modest and cozy places. With years of experience and continued research, we have the expertise to transform a simple dining area into a warm and inviting bistrot. We will make sure all your requirements are looked into and executed exquisitely. At Umber Frame Studio, we will work till you are satisfied with the ambience and the aura of the space. Along with the above, we will also give you the competitive prices in the market making this the best deal for your buck.

Quick Service Design

A quick service restaurant design needs to be very intelligently thought out. While the seating area needs to be minimalistic, the movement area should be spacious and well organized to accommodate fast moving crowds and quick service. Our top interior designers come up with multiple solutions and options that make the best use of the square footage based on what the client wants to accomplish. We will renovate the area to match the theme and aesthetics you have in mind. We will also work on the parking and drive-through spaces and provide you with the most optimized as well as eco-friendly results.

We strive to achieve perfection in design through innovation & creativity.
